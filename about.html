About Sam & Ari Kids Club

About Sam & Ari Kids Club

Sam & Ari Kids Club blends structured growth with joyful experiences, nurturing confident, creative and socially intelligent children. Through immersive programs, movement activities and guided play, we help children grow into curious leaders who enjoy learning.

Our approach combines leadership building, teamwork, emotional intelligence and active play, ensuring children grow not just academically, but socially and personally.

About the Founder

Amara Bennett

Amara Bennett is the visionary founder of Sam & Ari Kids Club. With over a decade of experience in youth development and structured programming, she created the club to combine intentional growth with joyful childhood experiences.

Her philosophy is simple: children thrive when creativity, movement and leadership development are nurtured together. Sam & Ari was built to be safe, structured and inspiring.
